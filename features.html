OVERVIEW

The 2021 Lexus IS 350 F-Sport combines luxury and performance with a 311-hp V6 engine, dynamic handling, sport-tuned suspension, premium materials, cutting-edge technology, and top-tier safety features

PERFORMANCE

Engine and Acceleration

The Lexus IS 350 F-Sport’s 3.5L V6 makes 311 hp and hits 0–60 mph in 5.6 seconds, delivering smooth and responsive power.

Transmission and Drivetrain

The RWD model uses an 8-speed automatic, while the AWD version has a 6-speed. Both deliver smooth, responsive shifts for strong performance and daily comfort.

Suspension and Handling

The F-Sport trim features Adaptive Variable Suspension that adjusts to road and driver input, paired with a stiff chassis for sharp cornering, minimal body roll, and a smooth, controlled ride.

Brakes

Large ventilated disc brakes (13.1" front, 11.7" rear) provide strong stopping power and solid pedal feel, enhancing confident driving dynamics.

Aerodynamic

The IS 350 F-Sport’s sharp lines and low stance improve airflow for better stability. Its front bumper and underbody panels reduce drag, boosting efficiency and handling.

SAFETY

Lexus Safety System+ 2.5

The IS 350 F-Sport includes Lexus Safety System+ 2.5, featuring a Pre-Collision System with pedestrian detection to help prevent frontal collisions.

Lane Assistance

Lane Tracing Assist and Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist help keep the car centered, gently correcting if it drifts to enhance highway safety.

Adaptive Cruise Control

Dynamic Radar Cruise Control maintains a safe distance, can stop the car fully, and resume automatically—ideal for traffic and long drives.

Automatic High Beams

Automatic High Beams switch between high and low based on traffic, enhancing night visibility without blinding other drivers.

DESIGN AND INTERIOR

F-Sport Styling

The IS 350 F-Sport stands out with a bold design—featuring a mesh spindle grille, large air intakes, and unique 19-inch wheels for a more aggressive look.

Driver-Focused Cabin

The cabin is sport-focused with bolstered F-Sport seats, a leather-wrapped wheel, aluminum trim, and an LFA-inspired digital cluster for a true performance feel.

TECHNOLOGY

Infotainment System

The IS 350 F-Sport features an 8- or 10.3-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and Alexa, offering a responsive, easy-to-use interface.

Audio and Connectivity

The optional Mark Levinson sound system offers rich audio quality, while USB ports, Bluetooth, and voice control enable easy, hands-free use.
